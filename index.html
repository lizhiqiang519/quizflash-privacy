QuizFlash Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 11, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how QuizFlash ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information We Collect

  • Selected Text: When you use QuizFlash, we process the text you select on webpages to generate multiple-choice questions.
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage data to improve our service.
  • Storage Data: We store your preferences and generated questions locally in your browser.

How We Use Information

  • To generate multiple-choice questions from your selected text
  • To improve our question generation algorithms
  • To provide you with a personalized learning experience

Third-Party Services

QuizFlash uses the following third-party services:

  • AI APIs (OpenAI, Anthropic) to process selected text and generate questions
  • These services receive only the text you select, with no personal identifiers
  • These services are subject to their own privacy policies

Data Storage

  • All generated questions and user preferences are stored locally in your browser
  • You can delete this data at any time through the extension settings

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your data with third parties except as necessary to provide our service (i.e., sending selected text to AI APIs for question generation).

Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete all stored data through the extension settings
  • Uninstall the extension at any time to stop all data collection

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
